Internet of
Things (IoT)

Information and communication Technology play an important role to support, enhance and develop a better method to transfer the information.

Enabling education
through ICT

IoT (Internet of Things) is basically an ecosystem that connect physical object to the internet so that one can access these objects remotely. In IoT, the word “Things” can be an automobile with built-in sensor or it can be a person with heart monitor. An IP(Internet Protocol) address is assigned to the object, then data is gathered or fetched from the object. Further collected data is transferred over the communication network without any intervention or manual assistance. Use of embedded technology make the physical objects interactive with their internal states, with the external environment and further make them intelligence.

Why we need IoT

If human being had computers which knew everything that is needed to know about physical objects, then human being would be easily count and track each and everything. It would further help in reducing the waste, cost and loss. We can easily judge or take decision to repair, replace the objects and can gather information like hearing or smell without extra circuitry. One can conclude that “IoT makes the objects or devices human independent in a way that it can observe, identify and understand the surrounding or situation.
